What is ANN?

Artificial Neural Networks are computing systems inspired by biological neural networks, fundamental to modern AI.

What is LLM?

Large Language Models are AI models trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human language.

What is GPT?

Generative Pre-trained Transformers are a type of LLM, known for their capability to generate coherent and contextually relevant text.

When did GPT start?

GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) is based on the Transformer architecture, which was introduced in the seminal paper "Attention Is All You Need" by Vaswani et al., published in June 2017. This paper laid the foundation for models like GPT by introducing the self‑attention mechanism that allows models to process and generate text more efficiently.

Self-attention formula:

Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax((QKᵀ) / √dₖ) V

What is Custom GPTs?

Custom GPTs are specialized versions of the GPT models tailored for specific tasks or industries by fine-tuning them on domain‑specific data. They are designed to meet the unique needs of businesses, allowing for more accurate and context‑aware interactions such as customer service automation or targeted content generation.

  • Instructions: Refer to the specific guidelines given to shape the GPT’s behavior and responses.
  • Actions (API): Integrations that allow the GPT to interact with external systems—retrieving data or executing commands.

What is DatabaseGPT?

gpt2ai is developing DatabaseGPT, a platform that integrates SQL databases with ChatGPT via Custom GPT Actions (API).

With 80% of the world’s data stored privately in databases, while ChatGPT uses data from the internet, DatabaseGPT uses data from your database. This allows users to query and manage their databases using natural language.
